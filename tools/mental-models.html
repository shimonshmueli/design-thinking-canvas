1.6 Mental Models心智模型

What it is

The internal explanations users carry about how something works — often incomplete or wrong relative to how the system actually works, but decisive for their behavior. A design that fights the user's mental model produces constant errors; one that matches it feels intuitive. Understanding the gap between the user's model and the system's model is a core Discover finding.用户对某件事物如何运作所持有的内在解释——这种理解相对于系统实际运作方式而言往往不完整甚至是错的，但却决定了用户的行为方式。与用户心智模型相悖的设计会不断产生错误；与之匹配的设计则让人感觉直观自然。理解用户模型与系统模型之间的差距，是探索阶段的一项核心发现。

How to use it

  1. Ask users to explain or draw how they think the thing works.请用户解释或画出他们认为该事物是如何运作的。
  2. Watch for errors and hesitations — they mark where the model and reality diverge.留意错误与犹豫——它们标示出模型与现实产生分歧的地方。
  3. Compare the user's model, the designer's model, and the system's actual behavior.比较用户的心智模型、设计者的模型，以及系统的实际行为。
  4. Record the user's vocabulary; their words for things reveal their model's structure.记录用户使用的词汇；他们对事物的称呼揭示了其心智模型的结构。

Watch out for

Correcting the user's model instead of learning from it. In Discover, a 'wrong' mental model is data, not a problem to fix.不要急于纠正用户的心智模型，而应从中学习。在探索阶段，一个『错误』的心智模型本身就是数据，而不是需要立刻修正的问题。
