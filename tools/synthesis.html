4.5 Synthesis (Concept Integration)综合（概念整合）

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

The convergent act of combining the surviving elements — selected ideas, prototype learnings, mockup decisions — into one coherent Solution Concept: an explicit description of the concept's structure and morphology, its functions and behaviors, its user interactions, and its form. This is the document/artifact pair that crosses the SC gate into Evaluate.一项收敛性的工作，把存活下来的各个元素——选定的想法、原型带来的经验、模型阶段的决策——整合成一个连贯一致的解决方案概念：明确描述其结构与形态、功能与行为、用户交互，以及形态本身。这正是穿越 SC 关卡、进入评估阶段的那份文档/成果组合。

How to use it

  1. Merge complementary strengths from different candidates deliberately (the Pugh hybrids).有意识地融合不同候选方案各自的优势（即 Pugh 矩阵中的『杂交』做法）。
  2. Write the concept down along all four SC dimensions — structure, functions, interactions, form.沿着 SC 的四个维度——结构、功能、交互、形态——把概念完整地写下来。
  3. Check coherence: do the parts serve one point-of-view, or is it a feature pile?检查一致性：各个部分是否服务于同一个视角，还是只是功能的堆砌？
  4. Trace each element back to a need in the brief; cut what traces to nothing.把每个元素都追溯回简报中的某项需求；无法追溯到任何需求的元素应当删除。

Watch out for

Frankenstein concepts — bolting together every stakeholder's favorite feature. Integration means one coherent whole, not a union of parts.警惕『弗兰肯斯坦式』概念——把每位利益相关者各自偏爱的功能生硬拼凑在一起。整合的含义是形成一个连贯的整体，而不是各部分的简单并集。

Worksheet

Apply Synthesis (Concept Integration) to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Make page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Make stage cards.运用「综合（概念整合）」处理你的项目，并记录你的发现。条目会保存在你的浏览器中，显示在「制作」页面『来自工具工作表』区块下，并纳入该页面的 AI 上下文，还可以被发送为「制作」阶段的卡片。
