Synthesis (Concept Integration)

What it is

The convergent act of combining the surviving elements — selected ideas, prototype learnings, mockup decisions — into one coherent Solution Concept: an explicit description of the concept's structure and morphology, its functions and behaviors, its user interactions, and its form. This is the document/artifact pair that crosses the SC gate into Evaluate.

How to use it

  1. Merge complementary strengths from different candidates deliberately (the Pugh hybrids).
  2. Write the concept down along all four SC dimensions — structure, functions, interactions, form.
  3. Check coherence: do the parts serve one point-of-view, or is it a feature pile?
  4. Trace each element back to a need in the brief; cut what traces to nothing.

Watch out for

Frankenstein concepts — bolting together every stakeholder's favorite feature. Integration means one coherent whole, not a union of parts.

Worksheet

Apply Synthesis (Concept Integration) to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Make page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Make stage cards.
