Technology Inventory

What it is

A systematic scan of technologies — mature, emerging, and adjacent-industry — that could serve the problem. Ideation grounded in what's actually becoming possible produces different (often better) options than ideation from imagination alone: many breakthrough products are known needs meeting newly available technology.

How to use it

  1. List technologies relevant to the problem's functions: sensing, connecting, computing, materials, delivery.
  2. For each, note maturity, cost trajectory, and who is using it in other industries.
  3. Ask the crossover question: what does an adjacent industry do routinely that would be novel here?
  4. Pair each promising technology with a user need from the brief — technology without a need is a solution looking for a problem.

Watch out for

Technology push: falling for an exciting technology and bending the problem to fit it. The brief stays in charge.

Worksheet

Apply Technology Inventory to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Ideate page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Ideate stage cards.
