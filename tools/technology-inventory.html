3.4 Technology Inventory技术盘点

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

A systematic scan of technologies — mature, emerging, and adjacent-industry — that could serve the problem. Ideation grounded in what's actually becoming possible produces different (often better) options than ideation from imagination alone: many breakthrough products are known needs meeting newly available technology.系统性地扫描可能服务于该问题的技术——成熟技术、新兴技术，以及相邻行业的技术。基于『实际正在变得可行』的技术来构思，比单纯凭想象构思能产生不同（通常也更好）的选项：许多突破性产品，正是已知需求与新近可用技术的相遇。

How to use it

  1. List technologies relevant to the problem's functions: sensing, connecting, computing, materials, delivery.列出与该问题所涉功能相关的技术：感知、连接、计算、材料、交付方式。
  2. For each, note maturity, cost trajectory, and who is using it in other industries.针对每项技术，记录其成熟度、成本变化趋势，以及其他行业中有谁在使用它。
  3. Ask the crossover question: what does an adjacent industry do routinely that would be novel here?提出跨界问题：相邻行业习以为常的做法，放到这里会不会是一种新颖的方式？
  4. Pair each promising technology with a user need from the brief — technology without a need is a solution looking for a problem.把每一项有潜力的技术与简报中的某个用户需求配对——没有需求支撑的技术，只是一个到处找问题的方案。

Watch out for

Technology push: falling for an exciting technology and bending the problem to fit it. The brief stays in charge.警惕『技术推动』陷阱：被某项令人兴奋的技术吸引，转而扭曲问题去迁就它。始终应由简报来主导。

Worksheet

Apply Technology Inventory to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Ideate page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Ideate stage cards.运用「技术盘点」处理你的项目，并记录你的发现。条目会保存在你的浏览器中，显示在「构思」页面『来自工具工作表』区块下，并纳入该页面的 AI 上下文，还可以被发送为「构思」阶段的卡片。
