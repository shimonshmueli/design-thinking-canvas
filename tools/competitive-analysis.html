1.7 Competitive Analysis竞争分析

What it is

A systematic look at how existing solutions — direct competitors, substitutes, and workarounds — address the problem space today. The goal in Discover is not to copy features but to learn what users already have, where current solutions fail them, and which needs remain unserved.系统性地考察现有方案——直接竞争者、替代品与变通做法——目前是如何应对该问题领域的。在探索阶段，目标不是照搬功能，而是了解用户目前已经拥有什么、现有方案在哪些地方让他们失望，以及哪些需求仍未被满足。

How to use it

  1. Inventory direct competitors, indirect substitutes, and the do-nothing / manual workaround option.盘点直接竞争者、间接替代品，以及『什么都不做／手动变通』这一选项。
  2. Evaluate each against user needs, not feature checklists.以用户需求而非功能清单为标准来评估每一个方案。
  3. Try competitor products yourself where possible; note where they frustrate you.尽可能亲自体验竞品；记下哪些地方让你感到不满。
  4. Summarize the white space: needs no current solution serves well.总结出『空白地带』：哪些需求是现有方案都没有很好满足的。

Watch out for

Feature-race thinking — cataloguing what competitors have and concluding you need it too. Gaps in the market matter more than parity.警惕『功能竞赛』式思维——把竞争对手拥有的功能列一遍，就得出『我们也需要』的结论。市场中的空白，比功能对等更重要。

Worksheet

Apply Competitive Analysis to your project and capture what you find.
