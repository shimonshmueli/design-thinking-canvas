Customer / User Testing

What it is

Putting the prototype in front of real users and watching them attempt real tasks with it. The classic protocol: give a task, ask the user to think aloud, and observe without helping. Five users per round typically expose the majority of serious usability problems, so many small rounds beat one big one.

How to use it

  1. Recruit participants who match the target personas.
  2. Write task scenarios, not instructions ('you want to refill your prescription' — not 'tap the refill button').
  3. Stay silent while they struggle; the struggle is the data.
  4. Debrief, then fix and re-test — iterate rounds rather than perfecting one.

Watch out for

Testing on colleagues, friends, or anyone who knows the project — they know too much. And never explain the design mid-test; you won't ship yourself alongside the product.

Worksheet

Apply Customer / User Testing to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Evaluate page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Evaluate stage cards.
