5.2 Customer / User Testing客户/用户测试

What it is

Putting the prototype in front of real users and watching them attempt real tasks with it. The classic protocol: give a task, ask the user to think aloud, and observe without helping. Five users per round typically expose the majority of serious usability problems, so many small rounds beat one big one.把原型摆在真实用户面前，观察他们用它尝试完成真实任务。经典的做法是：给出一个任务，请用户『出声思考』，然后在不提供帮助的情况下进行观察。通常每轮 5 名用户就能暴露出大多数严重的可用性问题，因此多轮小规模测试要优于一次大规模测试。

How to use it

  1. Recruit participants who match the target personas.招募符合目标用户画像的参与者。
  2. Write task scenarios, not instructions ('you want to refill your prescription' — not 'tap the refill button').编写任务情境，而不是操作指令（应写『你想要续开处方药』，而不是『点击续药按钮』）。
  3. Stay silent while they struggle; the struggle is the data.在用户遇到困难时保持沉默；他们挣扎的过程本身就是数据。
  4. Debrief, then fix and re-test — iterate rounds rather than perfecting one.测试后进行复盘，然后修复并再次测试——通过多轮迭代，而不是追求一次做到完美。

Watch out for

Testing on colleagues, friends, or anyone who knows the project — they know too much. And never explain the design mid-test; you won't ship yourself alongside the product.不要拿同事、朋友，或任何了解这个项目的人来做测试——他们知道得太多了。而且在测试过程中绝不要解释设计意图；产品交付使用时，你不会跟着一起出现在旁边解释。
