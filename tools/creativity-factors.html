3.1 Creativity Enablers, Inhibitors & Facilitators创造力的促进、抑制与助推因素

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

The conditions under which groups actually produce novel ideas. Enablers include psychological safety, diverse perspectives, time to incubate, and clear constraints; inhibitors include premature evaluation, status hierarchies in the room, fear of looking foolish, and rewarding only 'realistic' suggestions. Designing the ideation session is itself a design task.群体真正能产出新颖想法所需要的条件。促进因素包括心理安全感、多元视角、足够的酝酿时间，以及清晰的约束条件；抑制因素则包括过早评判、房间里的地位等级、害怕显得愚蠢，以及只奖励『现实可行』的建议。设计一场构思会议本身，就是一项设计任务。

How to use it

  1. Set explicit rules that defer judgment — critique comes later, in Make.设定明确的规则，暂缓评判——批评留到之后的制作阶段再进行。
  2. Mix disciplines and seniority levels; have people generate ideas silently before sharing (avoids anchoring).混合不同学科背景与资历层级的人；先让大家默默各自生成想法，再分享（避免锚定效应）。
  3. Use constraints deliberately: 'how would we solve this with no screen?' provokes more than a blank page.刻意运用约束条件：『如果没有屏幕，我们该怎么解决这个问题？』比一张白纸更能激发思考。
  4. Watch the room: if only two people are talking, change the format, not the people.留意现场情况：如果只有两个人在发言，应该改变形式，而不是更换人员。

Watch out for

The loudest-voice problem: unstructured group brainstorms reliably converge on the first confident speaker's idea. Structure protects divergence.警惕『嗓门最大』问题：缺乏结构的群体头脑风暴，往往会不由自主地收敛到第一个自信发言者的想法上。结构化流程能保护发散性。
