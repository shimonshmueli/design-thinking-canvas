2.2 Internal Analysis (SWOT, Five Forces)内部分析（SWOT、五力模型）

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

Framing the problem relative to your organization's position. SWOT maps internal strengths and weaknesses against external opportunities and threats; Porter's Five Forces assesses the competitive structure of the market (rivalry, new entrants, substitutes, supplier and buyer power). In Define, these tools check that the problem you're converging on is one your organization can credibly and viably pursue.从组织自身处境出发来审视问题。SWOT 将内部的优势与劣势，对照外部的机会与威胁；波特五力模型则评估市场的竞争结构（同业竞争、新进入者、替代品、供应商与买方议价能力）。在定义阶段，这些工具用来检验你正在收敛的问题，是否是组织真正能够可信且可持续地去追求的。

How to use it

  1. Run SWOT specifically about the candidate problem, not about the company in general.针对候选问题本身来做 SWOT 分析，而不是泛泛地分析公司整体。
  2. Use Five Forces to understand whether solving this problem creates a defensible position.用五力模型判断解决这个问题是否能创造出可防御的市场地位。
  3. Feed conclusions into the brief's constraints and point-of-view.把分析结论纳入简报的限制条件与切入视角中。
  4. Revisit the original challenge-selection criteria: does this still fit our values, objectives, role?回顾最初的挑战筛选标准：这是否仍然符合我们的价值观、目标与角色定位？

Watch out for

Generic SWOT lists ('strength: our people') that could apply to any company and constrain nothing. Every entry should change a decision.警惕泛泛而谈的 SWOT 条目（例如『优势：我们的人才』），这种说法放在任何公司身上都成立，也起不到任何约束作用。每一条内容都应该能影响某个决策。

Worksheet

Apply Internal Analysis (SWOT, Five Forces) to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Define page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Define stage cards.运用「内部分析（SWOT、五力模型）」处理你的项目，并记录你的发现。条目会保存在你的浏览器中，显示在「定义」页面『来自工具工作表』区块下，并纳入该页面的 AI 上下文，还可以被发送为「定义」阶段的卡片。
