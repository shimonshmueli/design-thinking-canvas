Storytelling叙事讲述

What it is

Packaging research findings as narrative — a day in the user's life, a journey through the pain point — so the insight lands emotionally, not just intellectually. Teams and stakeholders act on what they feel; a well-told user story creates shared conviction about which problem matters in a way a spreadsheet of findings cannot.把研究发现包装成叙事——用户生活中的一天、贯穿痛点的一段旅程——让洞察不仅在理智上说得通，也能在情感上打动人。团队与利益相关者往往依据自己的感受行动；一个讲得好的用户故事，能以一份发现清单做不到的方式，凝聚起大家对『哪个问题真正重要』的共同信念。

How to use it

  1. Choose the one insight the audience must retain, and build the story around it.选定一个受众必须记住的核心洞察，围绕它来构建故事。
  2. Use a real (anonymized) user and real details from research.使用真实（匿名化）的用户及来自研究的真实细节。
  3. Structure it: situation, struggle, consequence — let the tension carry the point.构建结构：情境、挣扎、后果——让张力来承载要传达的观点。
  4. Deliver it wherever alignment is needed: kickoffs, stakeholder reviews, the brief itself.在任何需要凝聚共识的场合讲述它：启动会、利益相关者评审，乃至简报本身。

Watch out for

A moving story about an unrepresentative user can steer the whole project toward an edge case. Pair narrative with the pattern evidence behind it.一个关于非典型用户的动人故事，可能会把整个项目引向一个边缘案例。叙事应该与其背后的模式证据配合使用。
