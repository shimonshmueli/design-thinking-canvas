5.3 Expert Panels & Heuristic Evaluation专家评审与启发式评估

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

Structured review by people with deep relevant expertise: domain specialists, and usability experts walking the interface against established heuristics (e.g., Nielsen's ten), guidelines, and standards (accessibility, safety, industry regulations). Experts find different problems than users do — cheaply, early, and without recruiting.由具备深厚相关专业知识的人进行结构化评审：领域专家，以及依据既有启发式准则（例如尼尔森十大可用性原则）、指南与标准（无障碍、安全、行业法规）来审查界面的可用性专家。专家发现的问题与用户不同——而且成本更低、介入更早，也不需要招募参与者。

How to use it

  1. Use multiple independent evaluators; single experts miss things and have hobbyhorses.使用多位独立的评审者；单个专家会有遗漏，也容易有个人偏好。
  2. Give them the brief and the criteria, not a sales pitch.给他们提供简报与评判标准，而不是一场推销式的介绍。
  3. For heuristic evaluation, have each reviewer log violations with severity ratings, then merge.在启发式评估中，让每位评审者分别记录违反项并标注严重程度，之后再汇总。
  4. Check compliance against applicable standards (accessibility, safety, sector regulation) as a distinct pass.单独安排一轮，检查是否符合相关标准（无障碍、安全、行业法规）。

Watch out for

Expert review complements user testing; it never replaces it. Experts predict where users will struggle — users demonstrate it.专家评审是对用户测试的补充，绝不能取代用户测试。专家能预测用户会在哪里遇到困难——而用户测试则能实际验证这一点。
