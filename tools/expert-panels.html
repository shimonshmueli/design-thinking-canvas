Expert Panels & Heuristic Evaluation

What it is

Structured review by people with deep relevant expertise: domain specialists, and usability experts walking the interface against established heuristics (e.g., Nielsen's ten), guidelines, and standards (accessibility, safety, industry regulations). Experts find different problems than users do — cheaply, early, and without recruiting.

How to use it

  1. Use multiple independent evaluators; single experts miss things and have hobbyhorses.
  2. Give them the brief and the criteria, not a sales pitch.
  3. For heuristic evaluation, have each reviewer log violations with severity ratings, then merge.
  4. Check compliance against applicable standards (accessibility, safety, sector regulation) as a distinct pass.

Watch out for

Expert review complements user testing; it never replaces it. Experts predict where users will struggle — users demonstrate it.

Worksheet

Apply Expert Panels & Heuristic Evaluation to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Evaluate page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Evaluate stage cards.
