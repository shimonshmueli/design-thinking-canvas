Back-casting反向构思法

What it is

Ideating in reverse: define a desirable future state ('in five years, nobody misses a dose of medication'), then work backwards to identify what would have to exist — technologies, behaviors, services — to make that future real. Where forecasting extrapolates from today's constraints, back-casting frees the team from them.反向构思：先设定一个理想的未来状态（『五年后，没有人会漏服药物』），然后倒推需要具备哪些条件——技术、行为、服务——才能让这个未来成真。预测是从今天的限制条件出发外推，而反向构思则让团队暂时摆脱这些限制。

How to use it

  1. Describe the target future concretely, from the user's point of view.从用户的视角出发，具体地描述目标未来。
  2. Work backwards in steps: what must be true just before that future? And before that?一步步向前倒推：在那个未来实现之前，必须先具备什么条件？在那之前呢？
  3. Identify the earliest links in the chain — those are candidate solution directions available now.找出这条链条中最早的环节——它们就是当下可行的候选解决方案方向。
  4. Compare against forward extrapolation to see which assumptions differ.与正向推演的结果做对比，看看哪些假设存在差异。

Watch out for

Utopian futures with no plausible chain back to the present. Every backward step should be an answerable 'what would it take?'警惕那种无法与现在建立合理链条的乌托邦式未来。每一步倒推，都应该是一个可以回答的『需要具备什么条件？』
