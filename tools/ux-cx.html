5.4 UX & CX Evaluation用户体验（UX）与客户体验（CX）评估

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

Assessing experience beyond task success: the user experience (UX) of interacting with the product — clarity, satisfaction, effort — and the customer experience (CX) across the entire journey around it: discovery, purchase, onboarding, support, renewal. A concept can pass task-level tests and still fail as an experience.评估超越任务成功与否的体验层面：与产品交互的用户体验（UX）——清晰度、满意度、所需付出的努力——以及围绕产品的整段旅程中的客户体验（CX）：发现、购买、上手、支持、续费。一个概念可能通过了任务层面的测试，却在体验层面依然是失败的。

How to use it

  1. Instrument the full journey from the journey maps made in Discover — evaluate the same touchpoints.依据探索阶段绘制的旅程图，为整段旅程布设评估点——评估同样的那些接触点。
  2. Combine behavioral measures (completion, time, errors) with attitudinal ones (satisfaction, perceived effort).将行为指标（完成率、耗时、错误数）与态度指标（满意度、主观感受到的付出）结合起来。
  3. Look for experience cliffs: single moments bad enough to undo everything else.留意『体验悬崖』：某个单一的糟糕瞬间，足以抵消掉其他所有好的体验。
  4. Feed CX findings to the parts of the organization that own those touchpoints — many aren't 'the product'.把 CX 层面的发现反馈给负责相应接触点的组织部门——其中许多接触点并不属于『产品』本身。

Watch out for

Averaging scores across the journey. A 4.5/5 average with one catastrophic touchpoint is a failing experience — users remember peaks and ends, not means.不要简单地对整段旅程的评分取平均值。一个平均分 4.5/5、但其中有一个灾难性接触点的体验，实际上是失败的——用户记住的是峰值与结尾，而不是平均值。

Worksheet

Apply UX & CX Evaluation to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Evaluate page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Evaluate stage cards.运用「用户体验（UX）与客户体验（CX）评估」处理你的项目，并记录你的发现。条目会保存在你的浏览器中，显示在「评估」页面『来自工具工作表』区块下，并纳入该页面的 AI 上下文，还可以被发送为「评估」阶段的卡片。
