5.6 Stress & Quality Tests压力与质量测试

What it is

Pushing the concept to its limits: peak load, extreme environments, edge-case data, sustained use, misuse. For physical products this means durability and environmental testing; for software, load and performance testing; for services, surge demand. The question is not 'does it work?' but 'when does it stop working, and how gracefully?'把概念推向极限：峰值负载、极端环境、边缘情况数据、持续使用、误用情况。对实体产品而言，这意味着耐久性与环境测试；对软件而言，这意味着负载与性能测试；对服务而言，这意味着需求激增的情况。这里要问的问题不是『它能不能用？』，而是『它在什么时候会失效，失效得是否体面？』

How to use it

  1. Define the expected operating envelope from the brief, then test beyond it.根据简报定义出预期的运行范围，然后测试超出这个范围之外的情况。
  2. Find the actual breaking point, not just 'passed at spec'.找出真正的临界失效点，而不只是『符合规格即通过』。
  3. Observe the failure mode: graceful degradation vs. catastrophic, silent failure.观察失效的方式：是体面地降级，还是灾难性的、无声无息的失效？
  4. Feed limits back into the concept documentation and the Develop gate's feasibility assessment.把发现的极限反馈到概念文档中，以及开发关卡的可行性评估中。

Watch out for

Testing only at nominal conditions. Real use lives at the tails — the tired user, the slow network, the hot warehouse, the year-end load spike.不要只在标称条件下测试。真实使用往往发生在极端情况：疲惫的用户、缓慢的网络、闷热的仓库、年终的流量高峰。
