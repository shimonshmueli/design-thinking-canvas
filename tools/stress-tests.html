Stress & Quality Tests

What it is

Pushing the concept to its limits: peak load, extreme environments, edge-case data, sustained use, misuse. For physical products this means durability and environmental testing; for software, load and performance testing; for services, surge demand. The question is not 'does it work?' but 'when does it stop working, and how gracefully?'

How to use it

  1. Define the expected operating envelope from the brief, then test beyond it.
  2. Find the actual breaking point, not just 'passed at spec'.
  3. Observe the failure mode: graceful degradation vs. catastrophic, silent failure.
  4. Feed limits back into the concept documentation and the Develop gate's feasibility assessment.

Watch out for

Testing only at nominal conditions. Real use lives at the tails — the tired user, the slow network, the hot warehouse, the year-end load spike.

Worksheet

Apply Stress & Quality Tests to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Evaluate page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Evaluate stage cards.
