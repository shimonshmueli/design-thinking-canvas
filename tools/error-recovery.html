5.5 Interactions & Error Recovery交互与错误恢复

What it is

Detailed evaluation of the interaction layer under imperfect conditions: what happens when the user makes a mistake, takes an unexpected path, or the system fails? Good designs prevent errors where possible, make them visible when they occur, and make recovery obvious and non-destructive. This is where robust concepts separate from fragile ones.在非理想条件下对交互层进行细致评估：当用户犯错、走上意料之外的路径，或系统出现故障时，会发生什么？好的设计会在可能的情况下预防错误，在错误发生时让其清晰可见，并让恢复方式显而易见且不具破坏性。这正是稳健的概念与脆弱的概念分道扬镳之处。

How to use it

  1. Enumerate likely error states: wrong input, interrupted flow, network/system failure, destructive actions.列举可能出现的错误状态：输入错误、流程被中断、网络/系统故障、破坏性操作。
  2. Walk each one: is the error prevented, clearly explained, and recoverable without data loss?逐一走查：该错误是否被预防了？是否被清晰地说明了？是否可以在不丢失数据的情况下恢复？
  3. Test with users who are rushed or distracted — error behavior under pressure is the real behavior.让处于匆忙或分心状态的用户来测试——在压力下的出错行为，才是真实的行为。
  4. Verify undo paths and confirmation for destructive operations.验证破坏性操作是否有『撤销』路径与确认机制。

Watch out for

Only ever demoing the happy path. If the team has never watched the concept fail, Evaluate isn't finished.警惕只演示『理想路径』。如果团队从未亲眼见过这个概念出错的样子，那么评估阶段就还没有真正完成。

Worksheet

Apply Interactions & Error Recovery to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Evaluate page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Evaluate stage cards.运用「交互与错误恢复」处理你的项目，并记录你的发现。条目会保存在你的浏览器中，显示在「评估」页面『来自工具工作表』区块下，并纳入该页面的 AI 上下文，还可以被发送为「评估」阶段的卡片。
