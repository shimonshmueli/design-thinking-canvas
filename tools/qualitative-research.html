Qualitative Research定性研究

What it is

Structured firsthand inquiry: contextual inquiry (interviewing users while they work, in their environment, letting their actions drive the questions) and ethnography (extended observation of people in their natural setting). Unlike surveys, qualitative methods surface the 'why' behind behavior and reveal needs users can't articulate directly.结构化的一手调研方法：情境访谈（在用户工作的真实环境中对其进行访谈，让他们的行动引导提问方向）与民族志（在自然环境中对人们进行长时间观察）。与问卷调查不同，定性方法能揭示行为背后的『为什么』，并发现用户无法直接表达的需求。

How to use it

  1. Prepare open-ended questions but follow the user's lead — the plan is a starting point, not a script.准备开放式问题，但要跟随用户的思路走——计划只是起点，不是台本。
  2. Ask about specific recent events ('walk me through the last time…'), not generalities ('what do you usually…').询问具体的近期事件（『带我回顾一下上次……』），而不是泛泛而谈（『你通常会……』）。
  3. Separate observation from interpretation in your notes.在笔记中把『观察到的事实』与『你的解读』分开记录。
  4. Continue until you reach saturation — new sessions stop producing new themes.持续调研直到达到饱和——新的访谈不再产生新的主题为止。

Watch out for

Leading questions and confirmation bias. If every finding confirms what the team already believed, the research design was probably flawed.警惕诱导性问题与确认偏误。如果每一项发现都恰好印证了团队原本的想法，那么研究设计很可能存在问题。
