4.3 Prototyping & Rapid Prototyping原型制作与快速原型

What it is

Building preliminary embodiments of a concept to learn from — from paper sketches and cardboard mockups to 3D prints and clickable interface prototypes. The governing rule: build at the lowest fidelity that answers the current question. Fidelity should rise only as questions get finer.制作概念的初步实体，以便从中学习——从纸面草图、纸板模型，到 3D 打印件与可点击的界面原型皆可。基本原则是：以能够回答当前问题的最低保真度来制作。只有当问题变得更细致时，保真度才应相应提高。

How to use it

  1. Name the question first: what does this prototype need to teach us?先明确要回答的问题：这个原型需要让我们学到什么？
  2. Choose fidelity to fit the question — paper for flow, cardboard for ergonomics, code for performance.根据问题选择合适的保真度——纸面原型适合验证流程，纸板模型适合验证人体工学，代码原型适合验证性能。
  3. Build fast, keep it disposable, and put it in front of users early.快速制作，保持『可丢弃』的心态，并尽早展示给用户。
  4. Prototype in parallel: several rough alternatives teach more than one polished one.并行制作多个原型：几个粗糙的备选方案，比一个精致的方案能教给你更多东西。

Watch out for

Attachment. A prototype the team is proud of stops being an experiment and starts being defended. Keep them cheap enough to throw away.警惕『恋物情结』。一旦团队开始为某个原型感到自豪，它就不再是一个实验，而变成了需要被捍卫的东西。要让原型足够廉价，廉价到可以随时丢弃。
