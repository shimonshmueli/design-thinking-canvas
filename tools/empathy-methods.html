Empathy Methods & Tools共情方法与工具

What it is

Techniques for understanding users' experiences from the inside rather than at a distance: empathy by simulation (recreating the user's conditions — e.g., wearing vision-impairment glasses), by immersion (living the experience yourself for a period), or by action (doing the user's tasks alongside them). Supporting tools include empathy maps, which organize what a user says, thinks, does, and feels.从内部而非旁观的角度理解用户体验的技巧：通过模拟（重现用户的处境——例如佩戴视觉障碍模拟眼镜）、通过沉浸（亲身体验一段时间）、或通过行动（与用户一起完成他们的任务）来共情。配套工具包括共情地图，用于整理用户所说、所想、所做与所感受的内容。

How to use it

  1. Choose the cheapest method that gets you genuinely close to the experience.选择成本最低、但能让你真正贴近该体验的方法。
  2. Record observations in the user's own words — resist paraphrasing into your assumptions.用用户自己的原话记录观察内容——避免用你自己的假设去改写。
  3. Capture emotional texture (frustration, pride, workarounds), not just tasks.捕捉情绪层面的细节（沮丧、自豪、变通做法），而不只是任务本身。
  4. Debrief immediately: what surprised you? Surprise is the signal of a broken assumption.立即复盘：什么让你感到意外？意外正是某个假设被打破的信号。

Watch out for

Simulation is an approximation, never the lived experience — treat it as a source of questions to verify with real users, not conclusions.模拟终究只是近似，永远不等同于真实的生活体验——应把它当作需要向真实用户验证的问题来源，而非结论本身。
