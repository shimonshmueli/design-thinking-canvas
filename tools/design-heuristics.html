3.5 Design Heuristics设计启发法

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

Reusable idea-generation moves distilled from studying how experienced designers create concepts — transformations like scale it up/down, merge two functions, invert the interaction, make it modular, change what powers it, let the user reconfigure it. Applied systematically to a seed concept, heuristics manufacture variation instead of waiting for inspiration.通过研究资深设计师如何构思概念而提炼出的、可重复使用的想法生成手法——诸如放大/缩小规模、合并两项功能、颠倒交互方式、使其模块化、更换其动力来源、让用户自行重新配置等变换手法。将这些启发法系统性地应用到一个种子概念上，可以主动『制造』出变体，而不必坐等灵感降临。

How to use it

  1. Take an existing or draft concept as the seed.选取一个现有或草拟的概念作为种子。
  2. Apply heuristics one at a time and sketch the resulting variant, even when it seems absurd.逐一应用启发法，并把产生的变体画出来，哪怕看起来很荒谬。
  3. Generate many variants cheaply; judge none of them yet.以低成本生成大量变体；暂时不做任何评判。
  4. Keep the variants that expose a genuinely different approach, not just cosmetic difference.保留那些呈现出真正不同思路的变体，而不只是表面上有差异的变体。

Watch out for

Treating heuristics as a checklist to complete rather than a provocation to think. The sketch matters more than the coverage.不要把这些启发法当成一份『要打勾完成』的清单，而应把它们当作激发思考的工具。草图本身，比覆盖了多少条启发法更重要。

Worksheet

Apply Design Heuristics to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Ideate page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Ideate stage cards.运用「设计启发法」处理你的项目，并记录你的发现。条目会保存在你的浏览器中，显示在「构思」页面『来自工具工作表』区块下，并纳入该页面的 AI 上下文，还可以被发送为「构思」阶段的卡片。
