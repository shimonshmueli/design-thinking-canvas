Design Heuristics

What it is

Reusable idea-generation moves distilled from studying how experienced designers create concepts — transformations like scale it up/down, merge two functions, invert the interaction, make it modular, change what powers it, let the user reconfigure it. Applied systematically to a seed concept, heuristics manufacture variation instead of waiting for inspiration.

How to use it

  1. Take an existing or draft concept as the seed.
  2. Apply heuristics one at a time and sketch the resulting variant, even when it seems absurd.
  3. Generate many variants cheaply; judge none of them yet.
  4. Keep the variants that expose a genuinely different approach, not just cosmetic difference.

Watch out for

Treating heuristics as a checklist to complete rather than a provocation to think. The sketch matters more than the coverage.

Worksheet

Apply Design Heuristics to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Ideate page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Ideate stage cards.
