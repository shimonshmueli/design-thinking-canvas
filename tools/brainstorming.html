3.2 Brainstorming头脑风暴

What it is

Semi-structured rapid idea generation in a group: a focused 'how might we' prompt, a fixed time box, judgment deferred, quantity encouraged, and building on others' ideas ('yes, and'). Done well it produces a wide option space fast; done badly it's a meeting where people defend their favorite idea.一种半结构化的群体快速生成想法的方法：聚焦的『我们该如何……』提示语、固定的时间限制、暂缓评判、鼓励数量，以及在他人想法基础上延伸（『是的，而且……』）。做得好，能快速产生广阔的选项空间；做得不好，就变成了一场大家各自维护自己心仪想法的会议。

How to use it

  1. Frame a specific 'How might we…' derived from the brief — narrow enough to aim at, broad enough to allow surprise.从简报出发提出一个具体的『我们该如何……』——既要足够具体以便瞄准，又要足够开放以留出惊喜的空间。
  2. Individual silent generation first, then share and build.先各自默默生成想法，再分享并在此基础上延伸。
  3. Go for volume; capture everything visibly; no criticism, no ownership.追求数量；把所有想法都清楚地记录下来；不批评、不认领归属。
  4. Cluster and label the output at the end — it feeds directly into concept selection in Make.最后对产出进行聚类并命名——这将直接用于制作阶段的概念筛选。

Watch out for

Stopping at the first satisfying idea. Push past the plateau — the second wave of ideas after the lull is often where novelty lives.不要止步于第一个让人满意的想法。要突破平台期——停顿之后出现的第二波想法，往往才是真正新颖之处。
