1.3 Contexts情境研究

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

Studying where and when the problem occurs: the physical environment, the social setting, the workflow around it, the time pressures, the devices and tools present. Behavior that looks irrational in the abstract usually makes sense in context — people improvise around constraints researchers can't see from a conference room.研究问题发生的地点与时机：物理环境、社会情境、周边的工作流程、时间压力，以及在场的设备与工具。抽象来看显得不合理的行为，放到具体情境中往往就说得通——人们会围绕研究者在会议室里看不到的种种限制即兴变通。

How to use it

  1. Go to where the activity actually happens; do not rely on recollection in an interview room.去活动实际发生的地方；不要依赖在访谈室里的回忆描述。
  2. Document the environment: noise, interruptions, lighting, space, other people, competing demands.记录环境细节：噪音、打断、光线、空间、周围的人以及相互竞争的需求。
  3. Note workarounds and improvised tools — they mark where existing solutions fail.记下变通做法与临时拼凑的工具——它们标示出现有方案失效的地方。
  4. Map the before and after: what triggers the activity, and what does the user do next?梳理前因后果：是什么触发了这项活动？用户接下来又会做什么？

Watch out for

Observing a sanitized or performed version of the context — people behave differently when watched. Spend enough time that normal behavior resumes.观察到的可能只是被『净化』或表演出来的情境版本——人们在被观察时行为会不一样。要花足够长的时间，直到正常行为恢复。
