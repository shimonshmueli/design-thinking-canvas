4.1 Pugh Matrix (Concept Selection)Pugh 矩阵（概念筛选）

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

A structured concept-selection method from engineering design (Stuart Pugh). Candidate concepts are compared criterion-by-criterion against a datum (reference) concept, scored better (+), same (0), or worse (−) on each criterion drawn from the brief's requirements. The point is less the arithmetic than the disciplined, criteria-driven argument it forces.一种源自工程设计领域（Stuart Pugh 提出）的结构化概念筛选方法。候选概念逐项标准与一个基准概念进行比较，在每一项源自简报需求的标准上评为更优（+）、相同（0）或更差（−）。这个方法的重点不在于算术本身，而在于它强制形成的、以标准为依据的严谨论证过程。

How to use it

  1. Choose criteria from the brief: needs, constraints, success measures.从简报中选取评判标准：需求、限制条件、成功衡量指标。
  2. Pick a datum — often the strongest existing solution.选定一个基准——通常是目前最强的现有方案。
  3. Score every concept +/0/− against the datum on each criterion; discuss disagreements, don't average them away.在每项标准上，将每个概念相对基准评为 +/0/−；对分歧要展开讨论，不要用平均分把它抹平。
  4. Look for hybrids: a concept weak overall may carry one brilliant element another concept can absorb.留意可以杂交组合的可能：一个整体较弱的概念，也许带有一个可以被其他概念吸收的出色亮点。
  5. Iterate with the best concept as the new datum.以当前最优概念作为新的基准，反复迭代。

Watch out for

Score-worship. The matrix is a conversation structure; if a result contradicts the team's informed judgment, interrogate the criteria and weights, not just the winner.警惕『唯分数论』。这个矩阵本质上是一种对话结构；如果结果与团队经过充分了解后的判断相矛盾，应该审视评判标准与权重本身，而不只是质疑得分最高的那个概念。
