1.5 Customer / User Journeys客户/用户旅程

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

A visual map of the user's end-to-end experience across time: the stages they pass through, the actions they take, the touchpoints they hit, and how they feel at each step. Journey maps expose the pain points, gaps, and moments of delight that individual interviews mention only in fragments.以可视化方式呈现用户随时间推移的端到端体验：他们经历的各个阶段、采取的行动、触及的接触点，以及每一步的感受。用户旅程图能揭示单次访谈中只是零散提及的痛点、断层与令人愉悦的瞬间。

How to use it

  1. Define whose journey and what scope (one task? the whole relationship with a service?).明确这是谁的旅程、范围有多大（单个任务？还是与某项服务的整体关系？）。
  2. Lay out phases left to right; under each, record actions, touchpoints, thoughts, and emotions.从左到右排列各个阶段；在每个阶段下记录行动、接触点、想法与情绪。
  3. Ground every cell in research evidence, not team speculation.每一格内容都要基于调研证据，而不是团队的臆测。
  4. Mark the pain points and 'moments of truth' — these become candidate problem statements in Define.标出痛点与『关键时刻』——它们会成为「定义」阶段候选问题陈述的素材。

Watch out for

Journey maps built from imagination in a workshop room are fiction with good graphic design. Evidence first.仅凭工作坊中的想象绘制出的旅程图，不过是画得好看的虚构故事。证据优先。

Worksheet

Apply Customer / User Journeys to your project and capture what you find. Entries are saved in your browser, appear under "From tool worksheets" on the Discover page, feed the AI context there, and can be sent up as Discover stage cards.运用「客户/用户旅程」处理你的项目，并记录你的发现。条目会保存在你的浏览器中，显示在「探索」页面『来自工具工作表』区块下，并纳入该页面的 AI 上下文，还可以被发送为「探索」阶段的卡片。
