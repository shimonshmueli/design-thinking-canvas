5.1 Testing Methods & Criteria测试方法与标准

↓ Jump to workspace

What it is

The plan behind the testing: which methods (usability tests, A/B comparisons, field trials, technical benchmarks) answer which questions, and — critically — the pass/fail criteria defined before testing begins. Criteria come from the brief's success measures; defining them in advance is what keeps evaluation honest.测试背后的计划：哪些方法（可用性测试、A/B 对比、现场试用、技术基准测试）能够回答哪些问题，以及——至关重要的是——在测试开始之前就定义好的通过/失败标准。这些标准来自简报中的成功衡量指标；提前定义它们，正是保证评估诚实客观的关键所在。

How to use it

  1. Return to the brief: what did we say success looks like? Turn each into a measurable test criterion.回到简报：我们当初说成功的样子是什么？把每一条都转化为可衡量的测试标准。
  2. Match method to question: behavior → observation; preference → comparison; robustness → benchmarks.让方法与问题相匹配：行为问题用观察法；偏好问题用对比法；稳健性问题用基准测试。
  3. Decide sample, tasks, and thresholds before the first session.在第一场测试开始之前，就确定好样本、任务与阈值。
  4. Report against the criteria, including the failures — especially the failures.对照标准进行汇报，包括失败的部分——尤其是失败的部分。

Watch out for

Moving the goalposts after results arrive. If the concept failed the pre-agreed criterion, the finding is 'it failed', not 'the criterion was wrong' — reframe consciously, not conveniently.警惕结果出来之后再『挪动球门』。如果概念没有达到事先约定的标准，得出的结论应该是『它失败了』，而不是『标准定错了』——重新框定应该是有意识的选择，而不是图方便的借口。
