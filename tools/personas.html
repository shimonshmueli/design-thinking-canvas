Personas用户画像

What it is

Composite, evidence-based characters that represent distinct user groups — each with goals, behaviors, frustrations, and context drawn from Discover research. Personas keep the team designing for specific people with specific needs instead of an elastic 'the user' who conveniently wants whatever the loudest person in the room wants.基于证据构建的复合型人物角色，代表不同的用户群体——每个角色都有源自探索阶段研究的目标、行为、困扰与情境。用户画像能让团队始终为具体的人和具体的需求而设计，而不是为一个可以随意变形的『用户』——恰好想要房间里嗓门最大那个人想要的东西。

How to use it

  1. Derive personas from behavioral patterns in your research, not demographics.从研究中的行为模式而非人口统计特征来提炼用户画像。
  2. Keep the set small — 3–5 — and mark one primary persona the design must serve.画像数量控制在少数——3 到 5 个——并标出一个必须优先服务的主要画像。
  3. Give each one goals, pain points, context, and a telling quote from real data.为每个画像赋予目标、痛点、情境，以及一句来自真实数据、有代表性的引语。
  4. Use them actively: 'would Maria understand this step?' should be a live question in every later stage.主动使用它们：『Maria 能理解这一步吗？』应该成为后续每个阶段都会被提出的现实问题。

Watch out for

Fictional personas invented without research are stereotypes with names. If you can't trace a persona attribute to evidence, cut it.没有研究依据、凭空捏造的用户画像，不过是带了名字的刻板印象。如果某个画像属性无法追溯到证据，就应该删掉它。
