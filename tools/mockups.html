4.4 Mockups模型/效果图

What it is

Static, visually faithful representations of the concept — screens, physical form studies, packaging — that show what the thing will look and feel like without full working behavior. Mockups converge the concept's form, attributes, and design language, and give stakeholders and test users something concrete to react to.对概念在视觉上高度还原的静态呈现——界面截图、实体造型研究、包装设计——展示这件事物将会呈现出的外观与质感，但不包含完整的可运作行为。模型能收敛概念的形态、属性与设计语言，也能给利益相关者与测试用户一个具体的、可以做出反应的对象。

How to use it

  1. Move from low-fidelity wireframes/sketches to higher-fidelity mockups as decisions settle.随着决策逐渐落定，从低保真的线框图/草图逐步过渡到高保真模型。
  2. Use realistic content — real labels, real data — because placeholder text hides real problems.使用贴近真实的内容——真实的标签、真实的数据——因为占位文字会掩盖真实存在的问题。
  3. Review against the brief's brand/design-language intent.对照简报中关于品牌/设计语言的意图进行评审。
  4. Pair with prototypes: mockups answer 'is this right?', prototypes answer 'does this work?'与原型搭配使用：模型回答『这样对不对？』，原型回答『这样行不行得通？』

Watch out for

Polish too early. A pixel-perfect mockup of the wrong concept generates enthusiastic agreement to build the wrong thing.不要过早追求精致完美。一个像素级精致但方向错误的模型，只会换来大家热情洋溢地一致同意去构建一个错误的东西。
