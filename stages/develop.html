Develop开发

Weigh viability, feasibility, and desirability — decide whether and what to build.权衡可行性、可行性与合意性——决定是否要开发，以及要开发什么。

↓ Jump to workspace

Develop is the gate between a validated concept and a committed development effort. The classic innovation test applies: a concept worth building sits at the intersection of desirability (people genuinely want it — evidenced by Evaluate), feasibility (it can actually be built with available technology, skills, and quality — evidenced by prototyping and stress tests), and viability (it sustains itself as a business — pricing, cost, channel, strategic fit).开发（Develop）是介于『已验证的概念』与『正式投入开发』之间的一道关卡。经典的创新检验标准在此适用：一个值得投入开发的概念，处在三者的交集之中——合意性（人们是否真心想要它——由评估阶段提供证据）、技术可行性（能否以现有的技术、能力与质量水平真正把它做出来——由原型制作与压力测试提供证据），以及商业可行性（它能否作为一门生意自我维持——定价、成本、渠道、战略契合度）。

The honest outcomes here are go, no-go, or go-back: fund development, kill the concept, or loop back into the process with what was learned. Weak concepts that pass this gate on enthusiasm cost far more to kill later.这里诚实的结果只有三种：通过、不通过，或者退回重来——投入资源开发、放弃该概念，或带着学到的东西回到流程中的某个阶段。仅凭一腔热情就通过这道关卡的薄弱概念，日后叫停的代价要高得多。

Comes in

The evaluated Solution Concept, with evidence against the brief's success criteria.经过评估的解决方案概念，以及对照简报成功标准所形成的证据。

Goes out

A build/no-build decision — and, on go, a development plan that carries the concept's intent into engineering, production, and launch.一个是否开发的决定——若决定通过，则同时产出一份开发计划，将该概念的意图带入工程、生产与上线环节。

Focus areas

  • Desirability — do target users demonstrably want this? What does the Evaluate evidence actually show?合意性 — 目标用户是否确实表现出想要它？评估阶段的证据究竟说明了什么？
  • Feasibility — can we build it, at the required quality, with the technology and capabilities we have or can get?技术可行性 — 以我们现有或能够获取的技术与能力，能否按所需质量把它做出来？
  • Viability — does the business model close? Cost, price, channel, and fit with strategy and brand.商业可行性 — 商业模式是否成立？成本、定价、渠道，以及与战略和品牌的契合度。
