Reflect & Improve反思与改进

The reflective practitioner, team, and organization — improve the process itself.反思型实践者、团队与组织——改进流程本身。

↓ Jump to workspace

The Double Diamond doesn't end with shipping; it ends with learning. Reflection operates at three levels: the individual reflective practitioner (what did I assume, miss, learn?), the team (how well did our collaboration, methods, and decisions work?), and the organization (what should change in how we select challenges, resource projects, and run this process next time?).双钻石流程并不是以交付上线而结束，而是以学习而结束。反思在三个层面上展开：个人层面的反思型实践者（我做了哪些假设？错过了什么？学到了什么？）、团队层面（我们的协作、方法与决策效果如何？），以及组织层面（下一次在挑战筛选、项目资源配置与流程执行方式上，应该做出哪些改变？）。

The output is concrete improvement, not a feelings circle: changes to team practices, method choices, and process design that make the next pass through the diamonds better — and a better next release of the product itself.这一阶段的产出应该是具体的改进，而不是一场『谈感受』的分享会：对团队实践、方法选择与流程设计做出的改变，让下一轮双钻石流程走得更好——也让产品本身的下一版发布变得更好。

Comes in

The full history of the project: decisions, artifacts, test evidence, and outcomes.整个项目的完整历史：决策、产出物、测试证据与结果。

Goes out

Documented improvements to teamwork, processes, and methods — and inputs for the next product release and the next challenge selection.关于团队协作、流程与方法的、有据可查的改进事项——以及供下一次产品发布与下一次挑战筛选参考的输入。

Focus areas

  • Practitioner — personal assumptions broken, skills gained, judgment calibrated.实践者 — 被打破的个人假设、获得的技能、被校准的判断力。
  • Team — what worked and failed in collaboration, method use, and decision-making.团队 — 在协作、方法运用与决策制定方面，哪些行之有效、哪些未能奏效。
  • Organization — process and portfolio lessons; what to change before the next cycle.组织 — 流程与项目组合方面的经验教训；下一轮周期开始前需要做出的改变。
  • Product — improvements queued for the next release.产品 — 排入下一版发布计划的各项改进。
