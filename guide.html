User Guide使用指南

This canvas walks a team — or a solo student — through the Double Diamond design process, from a broad challenge to a tested solution concept and a final report. You supply the thinking; the optional AI helps you sharpen and organize it. This guide is a friendly tour of everything the app can do. 这个画布带领团队——或独自学习的学生——完成双钻石（Double Diamond）设计流程：从一个宽泛的挑战，走向经过验证的解决方案概念，最终形成一份报告。思考由你完成；可选的 AI 帮助你打磨与梳理。本指南将友好地带你了解应用的全部功能。

👋 Throughout this guide you'll follow Team Nightingale — Ana, Ben, and Carla — a student team working on “Medication safety for home caregivers.” Their side notes show how each feature works in practice. 👋 在本指南中，你会跟随南丁格尔小组——Ana、Ben 和 Carla——一个正在研究「居家照护者的用药安全」的学生团队。他们的旁注展示了每个功能的实际用法。

1 · The Double Diamond1 · 双钻石模型

The process has two “diamonds.” In the problem space you first go wide (Discover) then narrow (Define) to a clear Problem Definition. In the solution space you go wide again (Ideate) then narrow (Make) to a Solution Concept — which you then Evaluate, run through the Develop gate, and Reflect on. 流程包含两个「钻石」。在问题空间中，你先发散（探索）再收敛（定义），得到清晰的问题定义。在解决方案空间中，你再次发散（构思）再收敛（制作），得到解决方案概念——随后进行评估、通过「发展」关卡，并进行反思。

Each of the seven stages has its own page with a workspace. The strip at the top of every page lets you jump between them, and ⓘ links open a glossary whenever a term needs explaining. 七个阶段各有自己的页面与工作区。每页顶部的导航条可在阶段之间跳转；遇到需要解释的术语时，点击 ⓘ 链接即可打开词汇表。

2 · Start: name your project2 · 开始：命名你的项目

The first time you open the app it asks for a real project title. Every page and every AI prompt refers back to it, so give it a specific name (you can rename it later using the ✎ Edit button next to the title). 首次打开应用时，它会要求你输入一个真实的项目名称。每个页面和每条 AI 提示都会引用它，因此请起一个具体的名字（之后可用标题旁的 ✎ 编辑按钮重命名）。

Team Nightingale南丁格尔小组

Ana names the project “Medication safety for home caregivers.” In a team, only the leader can rename a project; on a solo canvas you own it and can rename it freely. Ana 将项目命名为「居家照护者的用药安全」。在团队中，只有组长能重命名项目；在个人画布上，你拥有它，可以自由重命名。

3 · The home dashboard3 · 主页仪表板

Home is where you frame the work. From top to bottom:主页是你为工作搭建框架的地方，从上到下依次是：

  • Challenge — the broad territory you're taking on. You must write it yourself; the AI can only rephrase it for clarity, never invent it.挑战——你要投入的宽泛领域。必须由你亲自撰写；AI 只能帮你改写以更清晰，绝不会替你构思。
  • Challenge selection worksheet — a fit assessment (values, objectives, role, SWOT) that ends in a decision: accept, scope down, or reject. It's collapsible so the page stays light once you've filled it in.挑战选择工作表——一次契合度评估（价值观、目标、角色、SWOT），最终形成决定：接受、缩小范围或拒绝。它可折叠，填写后页面会更简洁。
  • Foundations — the theme/category and the thinking modes (divergent vs. convergent) that frame the process.基础——主题/品类，以及构成流程框架的思维模式（发散与收敛）。
  • Stage summary board — read-only previews of each stage. The real work happens on the stage pages; click a column to open its workspace.阶段总览面板——各阶段的只读预览。实际工作在阶段页面进行；点击某一列即可打开其工作区。

A dismissible welcome card at the very top recaps these three steps the first time you visit.首次访问时，页面最上方会有一张可关闭的欢迎卡片，概括这三个步骤。

4 · Working a stage4 · 进行某个阶段

Open any stage (e.g. Discover) and scroll to — or click ↓ Jump to workspace for — the Workspace. Type a note and press + Add card. Cards are saved automatically in your browser.打开任意阶段（如「探索」），滚动到——或点击 ↓ 跳到工作区——工作区。输入一条笔记并点击 + 添加卡片。卡片会自动保存在你的浏览器中。

  • Click a card's text to edit it. Clearing it won't delete it — removing a card is only ever done with its ✕ button.点击卡片文字即可编辑。清空文字并不会删除它——删除卡片只能通过其 ✕ 按钮完成。
  • In a team, each card shows its author's name; hover the name to see that teammate's “about yourself” note.在团队中，每张卡片都会显示作者姓名；将鼠标悬停在姓名上，可查看该队友的「关于你自己」说明。

5 · Tools & worksheets5 · 工具与工作表

Each stage lists relevant tools and methods (personas, competitive analysis, prototyping, and so on). Open a tool to read how it works and to keep a worksheet of findings. Any worksheet entry can be sent up into the stage as a card with Send to stage, and worksheet notes also feed the AI's context on that stage.每个阶段都会列出相关的工具与方法（人物画像、竞争分析、原型制作等）。打开某个工具，可阅读其用法并保存一份发现的工作表。任何工作表条目都可通过 发送到阶段 作为卡片送入该阶段；工作表中的笔记也会纳入该阶段 AI 的上下文。

Team Nightingale南丁格尔小组

Ben fills the Competitive Analysis worksheet with pill-organizer apps, then promotes the two sharpest findings into Discover cards for the whole team to build on. Ben 在「竞争分析」工作表中记录各种药盒管理 App，然后把两条最尖锐的发现提升为「探索」阶段的卡片，供全队在此基础上继续深入。

6 · The Problem Definition (brief)6 · 问题定义（brief）

On the Define page you write the brief: the problem statement, users & stakeholders, needs, a point-of-view, a How-Might-We, objectives, and success criteria. This brief is the hinge of the whole project — the second diamond (Ideate onward) gently nudges you to fill it in before its AI helpers unlock, so solutions stay anchored to a real problem.在定义页面，你撰写 brief：问题陈述、用户与利益相关者、需求、观点（POV）、「我们如何能…（HMW）」、目标以及成功标准。这份 brief 是整个项目的枢纽——第二个钻石（从「构思」开始）会温和地提示你先填写它，其 AI 助手才会解锁，从而让方案始终锚定在真实问题上。

7 · The AI assistant (optional)7 · AI 助手（可选）

The ✦ buttons are optional and bring-your-own-key. Open Settings → Identity & AI, choose a provider, and paste your own API key. The key is stored only in your browser and is never included in exports. Without a key the app works fully — you just do the writing yourself.✦ 按钮为可选功能，采用「自带密钥」方式。打开设置 → 身份与 AI，选择一个服务商，并粘贴你自己的 API 密钥。密钥仅保存在你的浏览器中，导出时绝不包含它。没有密钥时应用也能完整使用——只是需要你自己完成撰写。

The AI is deliberately a helper, not a decider: it can suggest cards, rephrase your challenge, or ask facilitator questions, but it never invents your challenge and never finalizes team decisions on its own.AI 被刻意设计为助手而非决策者：它可以建议卡片、改写你的挑战，或提出引导性问题，但绝不会替你构思挑战，也绝不会自行敲定团队的决定。

Tip提示

Fill in “About yourself” in Settings (e.g. “design student, new to strategy”). The AI uses it to pitch its suggestions at the right level for you. 在设置中填写「关于你自己」（例如「设计专业学生，刚接触战略」）。AI 会据此把建议调整到适合你的层次。

8 · Generating a report8 · 生成报告

The Report page compiles everything — challenge, brief, every stage's cards, and every worksheet — into one Markdown document with Mermaid charts. With an AI key it writes a polished narrative around your content (and flags gaps rather than filling them); without a key you get a clean data-only report. Download the .md file or copy it out.报告页面会把所有内容——挑战、brief、各阶段卡片、各工作表——汇编成一份带 Mermaid 图表的 Markdown 文档。配置了 AI 密钥时，它会围绕你的内容撰写一段流畅的叙述（并标注缺口而非替你填补）；没有密钥时，你会得到一份干净的纯数据报告。可下载 .md 文件或复制导出。

9 · Teams: work together9 · 团队：协同工作

Everything above works solo. To collaborate across devices, open Settings → Teams & Canvases (first set your name in the Identity tab — it becomes your team identity).以上功能都可单人使用。若要跨设备协作，请打开设置 → 团队与画布（请先在「身份」标签中填写姓名——它将成为你在团队中的身份）。

  • Start a team turns your current canvas into a shared project and gives you a 6-character join code. Use Copy to share it.创建团队会把你当前的画布变成一个共享项目，并给你一个 6 位加入代码。用 复制 分享它。
  • Join a team with a code connects you to that project. Everyone's cards sync within a few seconds.用代码加入团队，即可连接到该项目。所有人的卡片会在几秒内同步。
  • Each contribution stays attributed to its author. You can edit or delete your own cards; the team leader can delete anyone's. Nobody silently overwrites anyone else's work.每份贡献都会保留其作者署名。你可以编辑或删除自己的卡片；组长可以删除任何人的卡片。任何人都不会悄悄覆盖他人的工作。
  • The leader chooses the approval rule: leader (the leader decides) or consensus (everyone must agree).组长选择审批规则：组长（由组长决定）或共识（所有人须一致同意）。

Team Nightingale南丁格尔小组

Ana starts the team and sends Ben and Carla the code. They each join from their own laptops and start adding interview notes to Discover — everyone sees the others' cards appear live. Ana 创建团队，并把代码发给 Ben 和 Carla。他们各自用自己的笔记本电脑加入，并开始向「探索」阶段添加访谈笔记——每个人都能实时看到他人的卡片出现。

10 · Readiness & consolidation10 · 就绪与合并

When a stage's collecting is done, the team turns its many individual cards into one agreed summary — without the AI ever deciding for you:当某个阶段的收集完成后，团队会把众多个人卡片整合为一份达成一致的摘要——而 AI 从不替你做决定：

Collecting收集中 → Everyone marks “ready”所有人标记「已完成」 → AI drafts a summaryAI 起草摘要 → Team approves团队批准 → Consolidated & locked已合并并锁定

Each member ticks “ready” on the stage's team panel when their own cards are done. Then — per the approval rule — the AI drafts a consolidated summary, everyone sees it, and it's saved as a single attributed “Team (consolidated)” card only after it's approved. The leader can reopen a locked phase if more work is needed. Your individual cards are never replaced.每位成员在阶段的团队面板上，于自己卡片完成后勾选「已完成」。随后——依据审批规则——AI 起草一份合并摘要，所有人查看，唯有获批后才会保存为一张署名为「团队（已合并）」的卡片。若需要更多工作，组长可重新开放已锁定的阶段。你的个人卡片永远不会被替换。

11 · Multiple canvases & leaving11 · 多个画布与退出团队

You can hold several canvases at once — one per team you're on, plus any solo ones. The Teams & Canvases tab lists them; Open switches the active canvas, and + New solo canvas starts a fresh one. Joining a team always creates a new canvas, so your current work is never overwritten.你可以同时拥有多个画布——每个所在团队一个，外加任意个人画布。团队与画布标签会列出它们；打开 切换当前画布，+ 新建个人画布 开启一个全新画布。加入团队总会创建一个新画布，因此你当前的工作绝不会被覆盖。

Leaving a team (the button at the bottom of the team panel) removes you from the roster for everyone, and turns that canvas into a solo one that keeps only the cards you added yourself.退出团队（团队面板底部的按钮）会把你从所有人的成员名单中移除，并把该画布变为个人画布，仅保留你自己添加的卡片。

12 · Export, import & saving12 · 导出、导入与保存

Your work saves automatically in your browser (and, once you're on a team, in the cloud — the footer shows which). In Settings → Teams & Canvases, Export saves the current canvas as a .json file; Import loads one into a brand-new canvas so it can't overwrite anything you're working on.你的工作会自动保存在浏览器中（一旦加入团队，也会保存到云端——页脚会显示当前状态）。在设置 → 团队与画布中，导出 会把当前画布保存为 .json 文件；导入 会将文件载入一个全新画布，因此不会覆盖你正在进行的任何工作。

13 · Tips & FAQ13 · 技巧与常见问题

  • Switch language anytime with the EN / 中文 toggle in the header — it applies to the whole app.随时用页眉的 EN / 中文 切换语言——它对整个应用生效。
  • Is my API key safe? It lives only in this browser and is excluded from every export.我的 API 密钥安全吗？它只保存在本浏览器中，并且在每次导出时都会被排除。
  • Do I have to use AI? No — every feature works without a key; the AI only ever assists.必须使用 AI 吗？不必——每个功能都可在没有密钥的情况下使用；AI 始终只是辅助。
  • Lost a teammate's card? Nothing is deleted when someone leaves — their entries stay on the team, just no longer attributed.某位队友的卡片不见了？有人退出时不会删除任何内容——他们的条目仍留在团队中，只是不再显示署名。
  • Stuck on where to start? Name the project, write a challenge, open Discover, and add your first observation. The rest follows the strip at the top.不知从何开始？命名项目、写下挑战、打开「探索」、添加你的第一条观察。其余的按页面顶部的导航条依次进行即可。

That's the whole tour. Head Home to start, or open ⚙ Settings to set up your name and (optionally) an AI key. 这就是完整的导览。前往主页开始，或打开⚙ 设置来设置你的姓名以及（可选的）AI 密钥。
